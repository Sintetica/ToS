Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 16, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Discord application (“App”) collects, uses, and manages information. By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

The App may receive certain data through Discord’s API when you interact with it. This may include:

  • User Data: Discord user ID, username, and discriminator (for identification).
  • Server Data: Guild ID, server configuration, and role information (for features such as role management).
  • Interaction Data: Commands you invoke, button clicks, and similar activity needed for functionality.

We do not collect your real name, email, or IP address directly. Any such data is handled only by Discord, not this App.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide the functionality of the App, such as responding to commands or managing roles.
  • To store configuration settings so features persist across sessions.
  • To improve stability, monitor errors, and enhance the App over time.

We never sell or rent your data. Data is used solely for the App’s functionality.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Interaction data may be processed temporarily while the App is running. In some cases, certain identifiers (such as user IDs or server settings) may be stored in a database to enable persistent features. Data is retained only as long as necessary and is deleted when no longer needed.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to third parties. The only data exchange occurs between the App and Discord’s API, as required for the App to function.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from such users. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child’s data has been collected, please contact us to request its deletion.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data processed by the App. However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure, and we disclaim liability for unauthorized access beyond our control.

8. Your Rights

  • You may remove the App from your Discord account or server at any time, which revokes its access.
  • You may request deletion of data associated with your Discord user ID.
  • To exercise these rights, please contact us using the method below.
